Exploratory Notes & Essential Formulas

Magnetostatics deals with magnetic fields generated by steady (DC) currents. Unlike electric fields which originate from point charges, magnetic fields are generated by charges in constant motion and they always form continuous, unbroken loops.

1. Magnetic Field Intensity (H) vs. Magnetic Flux Density (B)
  • Magnetic Field Intensity (H): The "effort" or magnetizing force driven purely by the free source currents. It is independent of the material medium. Unit: Amperes/meter (A/m).
  • Magnetic Flux Density (B): The actual, physical magnetic field that results. It depends heavily on the material the field penetrates. Unit: Tesla (T) or Webers/m².
  • The Relationship: B = μH = μ_oμ_r H (where μ_o = 4π × 10-7 H/m).
2. Ampere's Circuit Law

This is the magnetostatic equivalent of Gauss's Law. It states that the line integral of H around a closed path is exactly equal to the direct current enclosed by that path.

  • Integral Form: ∮ H · dl = I_enc = ∫ J · dS
  • Differential Form: Derived using Stokes' theorem. It proves the magnetic field is rotational (has "swirl"). ∇ × H = J (where J is the volume current density).
3. The Non-Existence of Magnetic Monopoles

Because magnetic field lines never start or stop, the net outward magnetic flux through any closed surface is exactly zero. Every line that enters a box must also exit it!

  • Integral Form: ∮ B · dS = 0
  • Differential Form: Derived using the Divergence theorem. ∇ · B = 0 (The field is strictly Solenoidal).
4. Magnetic Forces & Lorentz Equation
  • Magnetic Force (Fm): A magnetic field ONLY exerts force on a moving charge. F_m = Q(v × B)
  • The force is always perpendicular to BOTH the velocity (v) and the field (B). It is maximum when the angle is 90° ($sin 90° = 1$).
  • Lorentz Force: Total force when both Electric and Magnetic fields are present. F = F_e + F_m = Q(E + v × B)
⚠ O'YEMI'S MAGNETOSTATIC TRAPS:
1. Infinite Wire Formula: $H = I / (2\pi\rho) a_\phi$. Don't confuse the $2\pi\rho$ with $4\pi r^2$!
2. True/False Pitfall: "Ampere's law is analogous to Coulomb's law." FALSE. It is analogous to Gauss's Law.
3. Work Done: A static magnetic field does ZERO work on a moving charge. It can only change the particle's direction, never its speed or kinetic energy!
